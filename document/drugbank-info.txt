DrugBank
========
 
http://www.drugbank.ca/downloads
  
Some of those data elements are in these tabbed text files:
 
drugs - list of the individual drugbank drugs
drugs and interactions - lists interactions with other drugs
mesh.txt - breaks out mesh categories for the drugs
pathways.txt - pathways for drugs
SNP-AES - SNP related Adverse events
SNP - mediated effects... pretty clear
target_2_drug - targets for a given drug with swissprot and gene referecnes
